We believe that one of the best ways to learn is by finding out how other people deal with the same questions and challenges we work with. One way we do this is by hosting regular roundtable discussions, where we invite other organisations and individuals working in online education and learning to talk about topics we all consider to be important. We broadcast and record these discussions so anyone is free to watch and share and learn from them too.
